Ordering Custom Solutions

 

    

 

A number of Experian services are considered to be Custom Solutions and as such fall outside the typical credit bureau response.  These can be preapproval services (known as either Instant Prescreen or Prequalification) as well as solutions geared to custom scoring or analysis (Premier Attributes, Stagg Attributes, etc).

 

Refer to the detail below which outlines the properties within the CreditSharp Connect namespace which should be configured in order to receive each of the various custom solution responses.

Prequalification

 

Assign the ExpProductCode.Preapproval enumeration to the ExperianRequest.ProductCode property.

 

When ordering the Standard prequalification product all that is required is the appropriate ExperianRequest.PermissiblePurposeCode (typically "3F").  

 

When ordering any other prequalification product supply the Experian provided values for the ExpInquiryOption.CustomRRKeyword and ExpInquiryOption.PrequalificationSolutionId properties.
