Attribute Factory Reference

 

    

 

To review Factory features, concepts or examples please refer to the Attribute Factory chapters.

 

If desired, Authorized User, Disputed and Lost/Stolen accounts can be eliminated for consideration in all these measurements.  Refer to the Attribute Exclusion Criteria topic.

 

 

Attribute Type Ranges

Description

001 - 199

COUNTS

Number of tradelines, public records, etc

201 - 299

TIME

Months since some event, average months since event, etc

301 - 399

AMOUNTS

Total balances, highest payment, etc

401 - 499

RATIOS

Percentage of open accounts, Amount of revolving credit utilized, etc.

500 - 599

LATES

Number of times 30 days late, Number of charged offs accounts, etc.

 

 

Attribute Categories

Description

TRL

Tradeline: measurements covering all types of tradeline (excludes collection accounts).

AUT

Auto: measurements restricted to installment auto history

BCD

Bankcard: measurements restricted to revolving bankcard history (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)

BFN

Bank Finance: measurements restricted to bank finance history

COL

Collection: measurements restricted to collection account history

CRL

Credit Line: measurements restricted to revolving credit line history

EDU

Education: measurements restricted to student loan history

FIN

Finance: measurements restricted to history with personal finance companies

FIX

Fixed: measurements related to all accounts with fixed monthly payments (Mortgage, Installment)

HEQ

Home Equity: measurements restricted to accounts secured by home equity

INQ

Inquiries: Measurements related to inquiry activity

INS

Finance: measurements related to installment accounts

MTG

Mortgage: measurements related to mortgage / real estate accounts

OPN

Open/30 Day: measurements restricted to revolving accounts whose balance is paid in full each month

PBK

Bankruptcy: Information regarding bankruptcy public records

PCV

Civil: Information regarding civil suit public records

PTL

Tax Lien: Information regarding tax lien public records

PUB

Public Record: Information regarding all derogatory public records

REA

Real Estate: Information pertaining to all tradelines secured by real estate

RET

Retail: measurements restricted to retail account history

REV

Revolving: measurements of revolving account history (excludes Open/30 Day and Credit Lines)

RPT

Report: Location for measurements that do not apply uniquely to another category

VAR

Variable: measurements related to all accounts with variable monthly payments(revolving, credit line or open.30 day)
