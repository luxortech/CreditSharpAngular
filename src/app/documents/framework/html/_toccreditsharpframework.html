CreditSharp Framework

 

  

 

The CreditSharp Framework is a powerful suite of tools for processing raw credit reporting information from the three major credit bureaus: Equifax, Experian and TransUnion.

Each credit bureau provides unique proprietary formats with specific interpretations of business and financial data. In general, they each provide data that represents the history and current status of a consumer’s credit related encounters. They differ in the way in which they report this information. In the spirit of normal competition and the natural evolution of business systems, these credit bureaus have, over the years, developed their own unique features and formats.

This uniqueness has created a market for systems providers to create solutions that provide a common interface for acquisition and analysis of data from any credit bureau. CreditSharp serves this role with a collection of robust and comprehensive tools to address the challenges in requesting, formatting and interpreting disparate sets of credit bureau data.
