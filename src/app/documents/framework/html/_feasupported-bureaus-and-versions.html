Bureaus supported

 

    

 

CreditSharp currently offers support for the three national credit repositories within the United States: Equifax, Experian and TransUnion; and the two national repositories within Canada: Equifax and TransUnion.

In addition to the above machine readable responses, each bureau's print-ready, human readable layouts can also be requested.  In most cases, each bureau also offers the option of receiving both a machine readable and human readable layout within the same response.

 

Bureau Products Supported

 

Equifax

Credit Report, Dtec Report, Finders Report, Military Borrower Check and Recovery Report

 

Note: Only the basic Credit Report product is available for use with Canadian requests.

 

Experian

Credit Report, Collection Report, Employment Insight, Military Borrower Check, Social Search, Instant Prescreen and Prequalification, Trended3D Express, STAGG Select along with various custom decisioning and scoring solutions (such as Clarity).

 

TransUnion

Credit Report, Collection Report, Military Borrower Check, ID Vision, Fraud Detect, Employment Report, Government Report, Fraud Detect, ID Report, ID Search, Identity Manager (version 1.0 and 2.0), SSN Report and SSN Search.

 

Note: Only the Credit Report, ID Search, and Model Report products are available for use with Canadian requests.
