ComPhone Enumerations

 

Note: All ConsumerPhone enumerations, with the exception of the ConsumerPhoneType, use the [Flags] attribute allowing the enumerated display to be comprised of multiple values.  For example, if the ConsumerPhoneContact display is 6, this means that both Evening (2) and Morning (4) are a good time to contact the consumer at the corresponding phone number.
