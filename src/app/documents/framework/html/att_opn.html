OPN (Open/30 Day) Attributes

 

    

 

The Open/30 Day attribute category includes values calculated with references to charges accounts whose entire balance must be paid each month.  They are considered revolving accounts but tabulated separately here.  For information which includes all types of revolving account detail, refer to the VAR attributes.
